Hosting & Domain

    The choice of hosting plan (shared, VPS, dedicated) and the provider will affect the price, with larger websites needing more robust hosting solutions.
  • Domain Name:
    The specific domain extension (.com, .com.ng, .ng, etc.) and the registrar's pricing will influence the domain registration cost.
  • Domain Name: ₦20,000 - ₦35,000 per year
  • Hosting Plan: ₦50,000 - ₦200,000 per year (or more for more robust plans)
  • Cloud/Virtual Private Server (VPS): (₦540,000 – ₦2,550,000 per year)
  • Dedicated Server: (₦2,400,000 – ₦6,300,000 per year)
  • SSL Certificate: (₦45,000 – ₦1,500,000)
